# Definitions to Know

- **Instantiate:**
    - Plug in values for variables *literally*
    - And then *simplify*
- **Expanding:**
    - $\forall$ (“for all”) becomes a bunch of $\land$ (“and”) statements
- **Trace:**
    - Tables of values for $n$ and $a$, for example (see notes)
